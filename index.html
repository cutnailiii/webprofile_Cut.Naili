SISFO STARTUP DAY 7.0

On June 14, 2023, we proudly participated in the SISFO STARTUP DAY 7.0 event with the theme 'Building Digital Economy Through Innovation And Public Private Partnership'. The Borneo Tech Studio Indonesia team presented our innovation in agriculture called "Kebun Komunal." We hope that our innovation can make a positive contribution to advancing the agricultural sector and supporting digital economic growth.

Kebun Komunal is an innovative solution in the field of agriculture. As a web-based application, Kebun Komunal facilitates farming communities in purchasing seeds and provides opportunities for substantial profits. Kebun Komunal enables users, or what we refer to as members, to make purchases and tend to their crops on our farms more efficiently, optimizing harvest yields by leveraging AI and IoT technology throughout the entire process.

As a member of a three-person hacking team, I played a pivotal role in the construction of the Kebun Komunal website, working under the mentorship of our professor during our Information System Development Project course. Our achievement includes the creation of an accessible landing page on kebunkomunal.id
